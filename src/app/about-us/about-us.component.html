What is CovidMeasures.info

Through institutional and crowdsourced data, we’re building an accurate picture of how this crisis has been affecting citizens around the world. On this platform, we look to engage visitors who are willing to share their knowledge, their experience, and their views on this crisis.

Don't be shy to contribute, we need your help to keep our data up-to-date and accurate.

Why This Matters?

We are all united together against a common viral enemy that has affected us all. Governments all over the world have responded, but not in the same way. While it is still too early to tell which measures have been the most effective, this platform aims to capture an accurate, reliable account of the impacts in each country through the eyes of its citizens.

 

It is our hope that all of us, as citizens of the world, can collect, compile, and critically analyze this data to make a difference in our communities.

Want to get involved?

Our platform measures and reports data. We need your help to keep the information accurate and up to date!

 

  • PARTICIPATE in our quick weekly question series. If you have more to say, feel free to contribute additional impact information.

  • POST our website on social media. Help us get more input and share this fascinating data with the rest of the world

  • PARTNER with us and let’s help each other out! We’re happy to work with institutions, organizations, and new volunteers.

The Data

We only use reputable sources of information from international government institutions, established media, or universities. When data is not yet available, we engage our user community to collect and curate new datasets and share them with the world.
