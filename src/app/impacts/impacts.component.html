How has your life changed during this pandemic? Join the discussion.

As each country responds to COVID-19 through different government measures, we're asking how society has been affected by these measures, and what we can learn.

To answer this question seriously, we need to understand first:

  • Understand how individuals have been affected around the world.
  • See the positive and negative effects of each government measure.
  • See the long term effects of these measures.
  • Weigh the goal of the measure against its effectiveness and consequences.
  • Document learnings/key take aways?

CovidMeasures aim to provide some facts and perspectives to help your thinking. Feel free to contribute to the debate.
