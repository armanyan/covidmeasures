As citizens we are questioning the necessity, effectiveness and the potential consequences of the introduction of new tracking or surveillance technologies as the result of COVID-19 outbreak.

To answer this question seriously, we need to understand first:

  • How is this information being collected?
  • How is the information being used?
  • Is tracking voluntary?
  • For how long are individuals being tracked?
  • Is the tracking effort transparent?

CovidMeasures aims to provide facts and perspectives to enrich your reflections. We encourage you to contribute to the debate.

This page is currently being written. We need your help to gather relevant data from around the world. Please add your contribution.
